The grammar

φ ::= 'false'    boolean false
    | 'true'     boolean true
    | ρ          atomic proposition
    | ⊙ φ        unary operator
    | φ ⊛ φ      binary operator
    | '(' φ ')'  parenthesized formula

⊙ ::= '!'  boolean negation
    | 'X'  Next operator
    | 'F'  Finally operator
    | 'G'  Globally operator

⊛ ::= '|'   boolean disjunction
    | '&'   boolean conjunction
    | '=>'  logical implication
    | 'U'   Until temporal operator
    | 'R'   Release temporal operator

ρ ::= [a-z][a-zA-Z0-9]*
