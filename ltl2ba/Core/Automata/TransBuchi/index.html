Module Automata.TransBuchi

Imperative representation of a Büchi Automata on transitions.

It uses the Graph.Imperative.Digraph.ConcreteLabeled functor to the implementation.

Vertexes are labeled with state where:

  • `Init s is the initial state labeled by s.
  • `Normal s is the normal state labeled by s.

Edges are labeled with FormulaSet.t where:

  • `Normal s is the normal transition labeled by s.
  • `Accept phis s is the accepting transition of all acceptance conditions Fα with α ∈ phis labeled by s.
